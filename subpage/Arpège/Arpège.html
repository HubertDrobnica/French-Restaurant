Note About Mizazur

Mirazur is a modern interpretation of classic French cuisine based on seasonal products from the Mediterranean. Her menu also includes fruit, vegetables and herbs from vegetable gardens for the needs of the restaurant. In his speech at the gala, Mauro Colagreco established values ​​in the foundations of modern France: brotherhood, freedom and equality. He emphasized that his restaurant is built by people from different countries, and the kitchen itself is something that knows no borders. He admitted that it was in France that he found his home and the opportunity, the author: realize his dream. At a press conference after the gala, Colagreco recalled that 13 years ago he founded Mirazur, took advantage of the help of two people in the kitchen and two services. Today it is made up of a 50-member team or, as he says, a family.

The Worlds 50 Best Restaurants ranking sponsored by San Pellegrino & Acqua Panna is, next to the Michelin guide, one of the most important and the most important combinations of the best restaurants from around the world. Initially, the list was simply an article prepared for "Restaurant Magazine", presenting the 50 best restaurants that are worth visiting. However, over the years, it has evolved into one of the most important events in the gastronomy world calendar, and its results affect the popularity of restaurants and their business success.
